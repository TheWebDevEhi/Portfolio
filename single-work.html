Work Description

E-Commerce Website Designed using Content Management Software; Wordpress with customisations done with PHP. This was created as a prerequisite for my training and also integrates checkout options and carting abilities using the WooCommerce plugin to securely and efficiently manage customers' info and orders while enabling the seller to easily scale up business by selling to those outside the geographical zone of the physical store, if available, thereby generating more sales and yield.
