Work Description

This is an alternative to a physical catalogue offered by businesses to show the perks of the business (which in this case is an events centre), show the services and facilities available and also can be customised to include the pricing plans. It is deliberately designed in a 2D structure, like a table, and not embedded in a carousel so as to give clients the feel of having a physical brochure in their hands and also enable them to easily locate their interests without having to view several unnecessary slides. This project can be modified to fit several industries and can be a stand-alone page or as an off-shoot of a main website.
